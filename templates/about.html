About PujaPath

Bridging ancient traditions with modern convenience to serve devotees across India

Our Story

PujaPath was born from a simple yet powerful vision: to make authentic Hindu rituals and spiritual services accessible to everyone, anywhere in India. In today's fast-paced world, many devotees find it challenging to locate qualified pandits or source authentic puja materials for their religious ceremonies.

Founded in 2025, we recognized this gap and created a platform that connects verified, experienced pandits with families seeking spiritual guidance. We've built a trusted marketplace where authenticity meets convenience, ensuring that sacred traditions are preserved while adapting to modern lifestyles.

Today, PujaPath serves thousands of families across 50+ cities in India, facilitating over 10,000 successful puja ceremonies and delivering authentic spiritual items to homes nationwide.
